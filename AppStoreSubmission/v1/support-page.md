## Common Questions

### Does One Line English require an account?

No. The app does not require login.

### Is learning progress stored online?

No. Learning progress is stored locally on your device.

### Can I change the app language?

Yes. Open Settings and choose English or Chinese. The app interface changes language, while the learning content remains in English.
